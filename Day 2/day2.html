MS Dhoni

Achievements

  • Captaincy: Under his leadership, the Indian cricket team won the 2007 ICC World Twenty20, the 2010 and 2016 Asia Cups, and the 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup.
  • Honors: He has been the recipient of numerous awards, including the Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna, the Padma Shri, and the Padma Bhushan.
  • IPL: He has also had a successful career in the Indian Premier League (IPL) as the captain of Chennai Super Kings, leading the team to three IPL titles in 2010, 2011, and 2018.
